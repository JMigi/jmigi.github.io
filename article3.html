Announcement Day 2018

By Emily Zybszynski

Posted 5/18/18

It’s the most wonderful time of the year… Spring Announcement Day! This morning, Wizards posted a video of Maria Bartholdi and Meghan Wolff (of Magic The Amateuring ) sitting down with Doug Beyer (Senior Creative Designer), Gavin Verhey (Senior Game Designer), and Dawn Murin (Senior Art Director) to discuss what’s in store for the next year. Terrible puns aside, the announcement gave us a pretty decent look into the next block in standard, as well as a peek into the new Battlebond set. Without further ado:


Battlebond


Announcement Day has brought our first spoiled Battlebond duo! Check them out:



So if you expected gritty, grizzled soldiers, I guess you were a bit off. Personally, I welcome the Power of Friendship in the meta. Also, note the “Partner with…” reminder text. Yes, these cards are commander legal and yes, they do work like other Partner commanders. And don’t worry about having to hunt down your duo’s second half for your teammate’s deck; the cards come paired with their buddy in the same pack!


Battlebond releases June 8, 2018.


Rereturn to Ravnica


Okay, so they gave it a different name, but that’s what I’d call it if they asked me. Which they didn’t.


The three part block will officially release November 5, 2018 with Guilds of Ravnica.



The set will focus on half of the classic Ravnican Guilds that we know and love:

  • Selesnya Conclave

  • Boros Legion

  • House Dimir

  • Izzet League

  • ❤️Golgari Swarm❤️


The second set, Ravnica Allegiance, will come out sometime in January 2019, and focus on the rest of the guilds. The devs seem to want to keep info about the as-yet-unnamed final set a secret, but as long as the pattern holds, we’ll see it sometime in April 2019.

In the same vein as the new block, we’re going to be getting a new product line starting in November: Guild Kits.


These kits are going to be theme decks, each revolving around one of the guilds. They’ll be released in two groups. The first bunch (corresponding to the guilds in Guilds of Ravnica) will be released November 2, 2018. The second bunch (corresponding to Ravnica Allegiance) will be out January 19, 2019.


Murin also gave us some insights into some of the aesthetics we can look forward to seeing. The setting will be Ravnica in autumn, with changing leaves and rain-streaked streets. Does this mean we’ll get to see Borborygmos cafe blogging and sipping a pumpkin spice latte? Given the updates to guild fashion, it doesn’t look likely. Murin hinted at a punky, aggro (ha) vibe for the red / green social anarchists. We also got a sneak peek at a sleeker Simic. Gone (or at least toned down) are the Crocanuras of old; the new Simic have moved onto more practical experiments. What exactly this means, time will tell. The Golgari, meanwhile, look like they’re set to fit right in with the new fungal meta; members of the Swarm have donned mushroom evening wear. The final guild Murin talked about, the Rakdos, has gone all in on their freakshow roots. Here’s to red / black debauchery!


But more importantly… Here’s to the Rereturn to Ravnica City of Ravnica!
