Starting out- Welcome to On the Play!

By Miguel Gatpolintan

Welcome to my site! What started as a school project combined with a passion of mine has turned into something that I would like to create something wonderful out of. On the Play NJ is a site catered to local NJ Magic the Gathering players of all kinds, from casuals to spikes. We hope to write articles that everyone can enjoy, and keep everyone up to date on the latest Magic the Gathering news. We'll slowly release more content as time goes by, and I'll also be updating the website aesthetics as well.

For now though, you can read our first article on Xenagos EDH. Feel free to send us any feedback on the website or our content, and thank you for visiting!
